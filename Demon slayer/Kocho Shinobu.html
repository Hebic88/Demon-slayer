Kocho Shinobu


  • Douma

Shinobu Kocho was a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.
She was a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the Insect Hashira of the Taisho era.
She was also the younger sister of the former Flower Hashira Kanae Kocho, and the adoptive older sister of Kanao Tsuyuri.

Personality:

It is later revealed that Shinobu's outwardly calm and tranquil disposition was worn as a mask, as she held an intense hatred for demons due to the loss of her beloved sister Kanae at a young age, along with the deaths of her parents, almost all of her Tsuguko, and the families of her apprentices at the Butterfly Mansion.

Fighting style:

Excellent swordsmanship skills: As a Hashira Shinobu was one of the most powerful and skilled swordswomen in the entire organization.
Her techniques were unique to her alone, but was as effective as any of the traditional swordsmanship styles. Her swordplay specialized in sword thrusting and piercing techniques in order to inject poison into demons.

Immense Speed & Reflexes: Shinobu possessed tremendous speed and reflexes enabling her to easily evade and out-pace demons, attacking them before they can even register what happened.

Breathing style: Insect Breathing

Insect Breathing is a breathing style created by Shinobu, who designed it to compensate for her lack of physical strength to cut off a demon's head combining the lethality of her poison, this Style allowed her to defeat countless demons.
